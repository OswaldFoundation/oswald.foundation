Accessibility

Oswald Foundation creates and promotes tools for accessibility like the Oswald extension that helps people with dyslexia or other learning disabilities browse the web.

Education

Oswald Foundation has partnered wih A4E to support the living and education costs of orphan children, and with Class Rebels to curate free educational content.

Empowerment

Oswald Foundation has partnered with Justice Adda to promote the development of interactive content to make law and justice more accessible, understandable and inclusive.

Valmiki

Oswald Foundation was founded with the development of the Valmiki, the Oswald extension, which is a plug-in for Blink-based web browsers like Google Chrome and Opera that changes the look and feel of the entire internet based on your preferences.

The extension is especially helpful for people with dyslexia, because it comes built-in with an option that adjusts the color-contrast ratio and typography to best suit the needs of someone with a learning disorder, based on previous research conducted by the W3C and the British Dyslexia Association.

AgastyaBeta

Agastya, our widget, is a powerful tool that allows web developers to implement accessibility features in their websites by adding just one line of JavaScript code. It comes with customizable modes like Dyslexia-friendly mode and Large font size, among others, and also allows developers to add their own custom-designed modes.

Currently in Beta, you can log in to the Dashboard to find usage analytics for the widget and your websites, including geolocation and demographics.
